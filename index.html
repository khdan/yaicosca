Who We Are

We are a group of traveler passionate about working, traveling and learning new languages

Since 2011 we have designed a 360 service focused on ensuring that the course you book to study abroad has the highest quality and every single detail will be looked after, from the program selection to the visa application, including accommodation and insurance. you can choose from a broad range of programs and destinations.

Visa Application

Depending on the duration of your program and the destination you choose, you might require to apply for a visa before traveling. In that case, we have a visa support team specialized in visa to study in countries like, Argentina, Colombia, Spain, Costa Rica to name a few.

Accommodation

If finding a place to stay in your new city is a problem, we can help you booking short term accommodation on student residence, homestays and hostels.

Programs

Whether learning a new language or starting a new career, we have a broad range of options in more than 18 countries around the world. We specialize in learning Spanish in Latin America or in Spain as well as studying in European Universities.

Support on Arrival

It is not just about booking a study and travel package, our service is meant to provide you with the tools to adapt as quick as possible to your new city with the help of our Ambassadors that will guide you once you arrive.

What We Do

We have everything you need to learn Spanish and have a great experience.

We did it once in 2011, and since then we have helped more than a thousand student to make their dreams come true, all of these by saving time and money with the guarantee that all the services and internationally certified

Study and travel

There is no better way to justify your new trip than learning a new language or skill. Speaking a second language and taking a leap year will take your career to the next level. Some of the programs include internships and the possibility to work abroad.

Work Abroad

Depending on the country you choose, you can apply for a study and work visa. This is a great way to practice your language skills while meeting new people in a new country.

University Exchange

Completing some of your credits while studying abroad for a semester in Europe is a great way to expand your horizons and living abroad.
